The Chocolate Story

Level 3 ⏱️ 2:30 🎧 American Accent ✍️ 12 Vocabulary Words
🎧 Original Audio
0:00 / 2:30
Speed:

Chocolate is one of the most beloved foods in the world. Its journey began thousands of years ago in ancient Mesoamerica, where the Olmec civilization first discovered the magical properties of the cacao bean.

The word "chocolate" comes from the Aztec word "xocolatl," which means "bitter water." The Aztecs believed that cacao seeds were a gift from the god of wisdom, Quetzalcoatl.

When Spanish explorers brought chocolate back to Europe in the 16th century, it remained a luxurious drink reserved for the wealthy. It wasn't until the 19th century that chocolate became the solid, affordable treat we know today.
